Intro

I am a researcher working at Samsung Research America in Smart System Group. My research interests cover distributed systems, cloud computing, and IoT.

Previously, I did my PhD at University of Chicago. I worked with Prof. Haryadi Gunawi in UCARE group. My thesis focused on improving the dependability of cloud-scale distributed systems. My expertise on this topic involves distributed concurrency bugs combating and scalability checking.
